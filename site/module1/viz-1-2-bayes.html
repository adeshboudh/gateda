GATE Angle — What GATE Actually Tests

NAT: Given P(H), P(E|H), P(E|¬H) — compute P(H|E). Always appears. Brute-force the formula; no shortcuts needed.

Trap 1: Confusing P(E|H) with P(H|E) — these are fundamentally different (medical test intuition above shows why).

Trap 2: Forgetting to divide by P(E) = P(E|H)·P(H) + P(E|¬H)·P(¬H).
