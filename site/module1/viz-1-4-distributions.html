GATE Angle — Distribution Cheatsheet

Know by heart: mean & variance of each distribution. GATE asks "E[X] = ?" constantly.

Bernoulli(p): μ=p, σ²=p(1−p).  Binomial(n,p): μ=np, σ²=np(1−p).

Geometric(p): μ=1/p, σ²=(1−p)/p².  Poisson(λ): μ=λ, σ²=λ (same!).

Uniform(a,b): μ=(a+b)/2, σ²=(b−a)²/12.  Exponential(λ): μ=1/λ, σ²=1/λ².

Normal: 68%/95%/99.7% within 1/2/3σ. Z = (X−μ)/σ ~ N(0,1).
