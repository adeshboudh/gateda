GATE Angle

Key trap: "arrange" → permutation; "select"/"choose" → combination.

Complement: P(at least one) = 1 − P(none). Often easier.

Inclusion-exclusion: P(A∪B∪C) = P(A)+P(B)+P(C) − P(A∩B) − P(A∩C) − P(B∩C) + P(A∩B∩C).
